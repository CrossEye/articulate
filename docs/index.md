Articulate Documentation
========================

Articulate is a collaborative editing system for hierarchical legal and policy documents.  It provides git-like branching, revision history, diff, and merge workflows, so teams can draft, review, and reconcile changes before publishing.

Articulate is **not** the canonical home of your documents -- it is the workspace where editing happens.  The official versions are generated as exports (HTML, TiddlyWiki, Word) and live wherever your organization needs them.
